Choose your gladiator

Gannicus

Gannicus is a former champion in the arena. His feats are skill, agility, speed and durability.

Agron

Agron is one of the strongest fighters in the show. His feats are durability and strength.

Crixus

Crixus is the Undefeated Gaul, another former champion. His feats are strength, defense and durability.

Spartacus

Spartacus is the strongest fighter and the current Champion. All the attributes can be called his feats as he is the most complete gladiator.

Onomaeus

Onomaeus is the oldest of them all and the former pride of House Batiatus. His feats are defense, skill, agility and durability.

Gaius Julius Caesar

Caesar is the only Roman that stands a chance against gladiators in the Arena. His feats are defense, strength, speed and agility.

You can attack or parry your opponents attacks, the one with the higher speed status will attack first. After winning a fight, your health points will replenish. Parrying is really good when your defense is high.
(You have 30% chance of parrying an attack and you only have a limited amout of time to parry.)
